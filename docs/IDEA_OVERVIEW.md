# Idea Overview — Pump or Rug Arena

## One-line pitch
A mobile game where users predict whether fresh launchpad tokens will **PUMP** or **RUG**.

## Why this exists
Most launchpad activity is chaos. People want signal, fun, and competition.

## Core loop
- Every hour, 2 tokens go live:
  - 1 from **pump.fun**
  - 1 from **bags.gm**
- Users have 10 minutes to predict: `PUMP` or `RUG`
- Round settles after 6 hours
- Leaderboards + streaks drive daily habit

## Mobile-first angle
- Designed for short sessions
- 2 taps per prediction
- Push notifications for round open + settlement

## Why this can be viral
- Degen-native format
- Constant hourly action
- Shareable score/streak screenshots
- Easy to explain, hard to master
